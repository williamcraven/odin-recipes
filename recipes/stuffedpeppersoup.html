Stuffed Pepper Soup

This healthy Stuffed Pepper Soup recipe is an easy weeknight meal! Instant Pot Stuffed Pepper soup with rice is a kid-friendly dinner that you can make in the crockpot or right on the stove!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb ground turkey. You can use beef or chicken if you'd like.
  • 1 medium onion, chopped. Any type will do.
  • 3 medium bell peppers, chopped. I've added up to 4 peppers, and it's yummy!
  • 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes include liquid.
  • 15 oz can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup dry brown rice short grain.
  • 2 cups chicken broth or water
  • 1 clove minced garlic
  • 2 tsp oregano
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 1/8 tsp salt

Instructions:

Pressure Cooker:

  1. Saut√© the ground turkey until no longer pink. Drain liquid, if necessary.
  2. Add all of the other ingredients to the pressure cooker. Close the lid and seal your vent.
  3. Cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.
  4. Quick pressure release. If you'd like to let the pressure release naturally for a few minutes, it'll be fine sitting there.

Crockpot:

  1. Brown the ground turkey in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add the ground turkey, broth, tomatoes, tomato sauce, chopped peppers and onion, black pepper, garlic, oregano, and salt to the Crock Pot.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours.
  4. Add your uncooked rice about 1 hour before cook time is finished. You can also cook rice separately and add it right before serving.
