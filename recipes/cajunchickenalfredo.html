Cajun Chicken Alfredo

This cajun chicken alfredo is a healthy dinner recipe the whole family will love. It's a spicy take on your favorite chicken alfredo recipe! This healthy alfredo sauce recipe is super versatile so you'll want to keep it on hand for all your pasta dishes!

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs. chicken breast
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp Cajun seasoning
  • 3 cups water
  • 4 cups whole grain pasta
  • 2/3 cup cheese parmesan
  • 3/4 cup cottage cheese 2%
  • 3/4 cup Greek yogurt 2%, plain
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Instructions:

Stove top:

  1. Coat your chicken breasts with olive oil and Cajun seasoning.
  2. Heat a large skillet on high heat on the stove.
  3. When the skillet is hot, sauté the chicken on each side for 2-3 minutes until it gets crispy. Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
  4. Add the water to the skillet and deglaze your pan.
  5. Add pasta to the water and give a quick stir.
  6. Move the chicken back to the pot and set on top of the pasta.
  7. Bring the water in the skillet to a boil, then cover the pan and reduce the heat to medium low, so that the liquid keeps simmering. Cook for 12-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the pasta and chicken are cooked through.
  8. While the pasta is cooking, blend together the cottage cheese, Greek yogurt, garlic powder and pepper to make your alfredo sauce. Set aside until ready to use.
  9. When your chicken is cooked through, remove and slice.
  10. Pour your alfredo sauce and parmesan cheese over the pasta and stir until it’s combined.
  11. Serve warm and top with the sliced chicken.
